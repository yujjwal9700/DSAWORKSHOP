You have given two numbers [a,b] find all the numbers which have 
an odd number of divisors

Input Format
A number T represents the number of test cases
Two numbers [a,b] represent the range

Output 
Count of the numbers having odd numbers of divisors in the range[a,b]

Example->1
Input
2
1 5
2 6
Output
2
1
